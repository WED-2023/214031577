🧔 About Me

🧔 Name: Lionel Andrés Messi Cuccittini

🎂 Age: Born on June 24, 1987 – currently 37 years old

🌍 Place of Birth: Rosario, Argentina

⚽ Life & Career:
Messi started his football journey at a young age in Argentina. Diagnosed with a growth hormone deficiency, he moved to Spain at 13 to join FC Barcelona. There, he spent over 20 years becoming a football legend. Today, he plays for Inter Miami and leads the Argentina national team with pride.

🏆 Prizes & Achievements:
🥇 7× Ballon d'Or
🏆 4× UEFA Champions League Titles
🏅 10× La Liga Titles with Barcelona
🌍 2022 FIFA World Cup Champion with Argentina
🥇 Olympic Gold Medalist (2008)

💙 Habits & Personality:
Messi is known for his humility, hard work, and quiet leadership. Off the pitch, he loves spending time with his family, playing with his kids, and supporting charities.

👨‍👩‍👧‍👦 Family:
Married to Antonela Roccuzzo ❤️
Father to three sons: Thiago 👦, Mateo 👦, and Ciro 👶.

Differences Between Web1, Web2, and Web3

Feature Web1 Web2 Web3
Content Type Static (Read Only) Dynamic (Read & Write) Decentralized (User Owned)
Control Over Data Site owners Big platforms (e.g. Facebook) Users via blockchain
Interaction None or minimal Likes, comments, posts Smart contracts, NFTs, DAOs

Use Case: Searching for “Side Effects of Medicine”

In Web1: You would find a static article on a medical site. No comments or updates.

In Web2: You might find a forum post, social media discussion, or blog with comments from other users sharing their experiences.

In Web3: You could use a decentralized health platform where verified users anonymously share their side effects, stored securely on the blockchain with transparency and privacy.
